DJ Ikamon

Ikamon takes a technologist's approach to performance: doing graphics engineering, rigging DMX lighting, and using unorthodox controllers and devices to bring you the coolest music experiences possible. They are primarily a House/Garage DJ but can perform open-genre sets as well.

Ikamon got their start during the 2020 pandemic bringing live music experiences to groups on VR platforms and has since become a virtual rave community leader, regularly hosting realtime VR events and experiences.

Services include:

-Live performances w/ or w/o visuals

-Remote consultation sessions to assist in creating visuals (for streamers & other DJs)

-Beginner DJ Lessons
